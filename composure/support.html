Frequently asked questions

Where is my data?

All practice and reflection data is stored locally on your iPhone.

How do I start over?

Open Today, tap the profile button, and choose Erase all data.

Is this medical treatment?

No. Composure provides general posture and self-regulation exercises. Stop and seek qualified guidance for persistent pain, numbness, weakness, dizziness, or marked asymmetry.
