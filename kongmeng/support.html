Common questions

Do I need an account?
No. There is no account system.

Does the widget need network access?
No. It uses bundled content and the same local daily picker as the app.

Can I switch languages?
Yes. The Settings tab supports Chinese, English, and bilingual display, plus simplified and traditional Chinese script options.
