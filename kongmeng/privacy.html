Privacy Policy

KongMeng is designed to work offline. We do not collect personal data.

The short version

  • No account or sign-in.
  • No analytics, ads, trackers, or third-party SDKs.
  • No server-side reading history.
  • No network requests are required for app functionality.
  • Favorites, preferences, and reading streak are stored locally on your device.

Data stored on device

The app stores reading preferences, favorite passage IDs, and a local reading streak using Apple-provided local storage. Widget data is shared locally through App Group storage. Deleting the app removes this local data according to iOS behavior.

What we do not collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, identifiers, analytics events, reading history, or device telemetry.

Children

KongMeng is a reading app and does not knowingly collect data from children or adults.
