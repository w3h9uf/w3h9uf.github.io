Tide

Screen Time pause

Pause before you open it.

Tide helps you add a short, private pause before distracting apps. Name what you feel, breathe for 60 seconds, and decide whether opening the app still matches your intention.

What Tide does

  • Uses Apple's Screen Time and Family Controls APIs to let you choose apps to guard on your device.
  • Shows a lightweight emotional check-in before a blocked app is opened.
  • Offers a 60-second reset ritual before you choose whether to continue.
  • Shows local patterns such as streaks, skipped opens, and feeling-map trends.

Privacy first

Tide has no account system and no advertising. App selections, check-ins, and feeling-map data stay on your device. Subscription billing is handled by Apple through the App Store.
