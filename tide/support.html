Common questions

Why does Tide ask for Screen Time permission?

Tide uses Apple's Screen Time APIs so you can select apps to guard and so Tide can show a pause before those apps are opened. Permission is required by iOS and can be revoked in Settings.

Does Tide see which apps I select?

The selected app tokens are handled on device through Apple's Family Controls framework. Tide does not run a server and does not upload your app choices.

How do I change guarded apps?

Open Tide, tap the settings icon, then use Blocked apps to update the selection.

How do I cancel Tide Pro?

On iPhone, open Settings, tap your Apple ID, choose Subscriptions, select Tide Pro, then cancel. Access remains active until the end of the current billing period.

How do I restore a purchase?

Open Tide settings and choose Restore purchases. StoreKit will refresh any active App Store entitlement.
