Stride Planner

Effective 2026-06-22

Privacy Policy

Stride Planner is built to help runners plan training while keeping health and workout data on device.

The short version

  • Stride Planner does not require an account.
  • Stride Planner does not include advertising or third-party analytics SDKs.
  • Apple Health access is optional and controlled by the user.
  • Health and workout data read from Apple Health stays on your device.
  • Stride Planner does not sell personal data.

Data used on your device

DataPurpose
Training goal, race date, weekly mileage, and experience levelUsed to generate and adjust the local training plan.
Running workouts and weekly distance from Apple HealthUsed to compare recent training load with the plan.
Resting heart rate, VO2 max, and sleep from Apple HealthUsed to provide recovery-aware coaching notes.
App settingsUsed to remember preferences and whether demo data is enabled.

Apple Health

Stride Planner requests read access to selected Apple Health data types: running workouts, walking/running distance, heart rate, resting heart rate, VO2 max, and sleep. Health access is optional. You can grant, deny, or revoke permissions at any time in the Health app or iOS Settings.

Stride Planner does not write workouts to Apple Health in the current release.

Storage and sharing

Training settings are stored in the app sandbox on your device. Apple Health samples are read from Apple Health for display and coaching and are not uploaded to Hashing Corp. Stride Planner does not run a server for user accounts, health data syncing, or analytics.

What we do not collect

  • No name, email, phone number, or account identifier is required to use the app.
  • No advertising identifier is collected.
  • No precise location data is collected by Stride Planner.
  • No contacts, photos, messages, or browsing content are collected.

Health and safety

Stride Planner provides general training guidance and is not medical advice. Consult a qualified professional before starting or changing a training plan, especially if you have a medical condition or injury.

Children

Stride Planner is intended for users who manage their own training. It is not directed to children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date above and describe material changes in release notes when appropriate.
