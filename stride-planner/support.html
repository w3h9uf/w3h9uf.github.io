Common questions

Why does Stride Planner ask for Apple Health access?

Apple Health access lets Stride Planner read recent running workouts, weekly distance, resting heart rate, VO2 max, and sleep so coaching can reflect your recent training and recovery.

Do I need Apple Health to use the app?

No. You can create a training goal and plan without Apple Health. Health data improves the daily coach recommendations.

Does Stride Planner upload my Health data?

No. Health data is read on device for coaching and is not sent to Hashing Corp.

How do I change my goal?

Open Stride Planner, go to Today, tap the settings button, and update your goal, current mileage, run days, or race date.
