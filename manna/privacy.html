Privacy Policy

Manna does not collect your data.

Last updated: July 4, 2026

No account

Manna does not require sign-up, sign-in, or a user account.

No tracking

Manna does not include analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, third-party trackers, or social sharing SDKs.

No server

The app does not send reading activity, favorites, preferences, or widget usage to any server. Reading content is bundled with the app.

Local storage

The app stores reading preferences, favorites, and local streak state on your device using system storage. The widget reads the same local preferences when available.
