Manna

Daily Bible verses in Chinese and English.

A quiet iOS app for one Scripture reading a day, a short reflection prompt, favorites, and Home Screen widgets.

One verse daily

Open the app to see one selected Bible verse that stays stable for the whole day.

Bilingual reading

Read in Chinese, English, or side-by-side. The first content set uses the 1919 Chinese Union Version and World English Bible.

Quiet by design

No feed, ranking, comments, ads, accounts, or analytics. Preferences and favorites stay on the device.

Widgets

Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets show the daily verse without opening the app.
